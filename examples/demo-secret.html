Demo Secret Page

This file is a placeholder you can encrypt and swap with your own celebration, announcement, or treasure hunt finale.

Once visitors answer your unlock questions, this HTML replaces the lock screen. Personalize it with photos, music embeds, or anything else you feel comfortable shipping in plain HTML.

Suggested Next Steps

  1. Edit src/_data/unlock.yml with questions and answers only your audience will know.
  2. Replace the content in this file with the message you want to reveal.
  3. Run invoke generate --password "your passphrase" --target examples/demo-secret.html to produce dist/page.enc.json.
  4. Deploy the Eleventy output in dist/; only people with the right answers will see your message.

Tip: keep a non-encrypted backup of your page somewhere safe. You can regenerate the payload at any time.
